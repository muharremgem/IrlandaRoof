Frequently Asked Questions

How can I tell if my roof needs to be repaired?

Here are some things that indicate the need for roof repair:

  • Missing shingles
  • Shingles that appear damaged
  • Holes in the roof
  • Discoloration of shingles
  • A depression or sagging area in the roof
What are the most common types of roof repairs that are needed?

Common roof repairs include:

  • Shingle replacement
  • Leak repairs
  • Flashing repairs
  • Gutter repairs and cleaning
  • Ventilation improvements
What will it cost me to repair my roof in Atlanta

The cost of roof repair in Atlanta varies depending on several factors:

  • Type and extent of damage
  • Roofing materials needed
  • Size of the repair area
  • Accessibility of the repair location
  • Labor costs
Will my roof repairs be covered by my homeowner's insurance?

Insurance coverage for roof repairs depends on:

  • Your specific policy coverage
  • Cause of the damage
  • Age and condition of your roof
  • Documentation of maintenance
What can I do to prevent needing future roof repairs?

Preventive measures include:

  • Regular roof inspections
  • Prompt repairs of minor issues
  • Proper ventilation maintenance
  • Regular gutter cleaning
  • Tree trimming near the roof
How often should my Atlanta home be inspected for roof damage?

Recommended inspection schedule:

  • Twice yearly (spring and fall)
  • After major storms
  • When buying/selling a home
  • If you notice any interior leaks
